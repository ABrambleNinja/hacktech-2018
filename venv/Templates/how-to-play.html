Rules of Espionage

  • A location is chosen randomly.
  • Each person gets a role associated with the location - except one.
  • This one person is a spy.
  • Your group gets one accusation per game. If your accusation is correct, and you have uncovered the spy, you win. Otherwise, the spy wins.
  • You may only accuse the spy if all but one person agree to accuse the same person.
